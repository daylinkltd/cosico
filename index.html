About COSICO

Empowering Consumers Across South India

The Consortium of South India Consumer Organisations (COSICO) is a unified platform dedicated to protecting and promoting consumer rights across South India. Our mission is to:

  • Advocate for strong consumer protection policies
  • Educate consumers about their rights and responsibilities
  • Provide support and resources to member organizations
  • Address consumer grievances and facilitate dispute resolution

Together, we strive to create a fair and informed marketplace that benefits all consumers in South India.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions about consumer rights and COSICO's services.

  • What are my basic consumer rights?

    As a consumer in India, you have six basic rights: Right to Safety, Right to Information, Right to Choose, Right to be Heard, Right to Seek Redressal, and Right to Consumer Education. These rights are protected under the Consumer Protection Act.

  • How can COSICO help me with a consumer complaint?

    COSICO can guide you through the complaint process, help you draft a formal complaint, and provide information on the appropriate authorities to approach. We can also offer mediation services in some cases to resolve disputes between consumers and businesses.

  • What types of consumer education programs does COSICO offer?

    COSICO offers a variety of consumer education programs, including workshops, seminars, and awareness campaigns. These cover topics such as understanding consumer rights, identifying unfair trade practices, digital literacy for safe online shopping, and financial consumer protection.

  • How can I become a member of COSICO?

    COSICO welcomes consumer organizations from South India to join our consortium. If you represent a consumer organization, please contact us for information on membership criteria and the application process. Individuals can support our work by volunteering or participating in our programs.

  • What recent policy changes has COSICO advocated for?

    COSICO has been actively involved in advocating for stronger e-commerce regulations, improved food safety standards, and enhanced financial consumer protection measures. We regularly participate in policy discussions and submit recommendations to relevant government bodies to strengthen consumer protection laws in South India.
